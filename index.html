① Pokémon Center TOKYO DX ⭐

Nihonbashi Takashimaya S.C. East Bldg, 5F · ~10 min walk / 1 stop from Ginza · open 10:30–21:00 · free entry, no reservation

The flagship Pokémon store in Tokyo — the closest one to your hotel and the “Pokémon park” for the kids. There is no Pokémon theme park in Tokyo; this is the main event.

⚠️ The attached Pokémon Café is closed for renovation through Jun 16 (reopens Jun 17), so café meals aren’t available today — but the store is fully open.

~10 min walk from the hotel · best in the morning, many stalls wind down by early afternoon

RECOMMENDED WITH KIDS + BAGS
Option A — Taxi door-to-door 🚕
~¥5,000–7,000 · 40–50 min

No stairs, no transfers, no hauling suitcases up to a monorail with two tired kids. For a solo parent + Andy + Katherine + luggage, this is worth every yen.

  • Ask the MIMARU front desk to call a taxi when you check out (~11 AM). Or use the GO taxi app.
  • Show the driver the address (screenshot this): 千葉県浦安市舞浜1-8 ヒルトン東京ベイ
  • Ride ~45 min via the Shuto Expressway along the bay — straight to the Hilton entrance.
  • Drop bags with Hilton Lobby Service (they hold luggage from 7 AM), then go enjoy the gap.

Option B — Train, direct line 🚆
~¥230 · ~35 min

Cheapest and genuinely easy if the bags are light: your area has a direct JR line to Maihama with no transfers. The only awkward bit is the final monorail/shuttle with luggage.

  • Walk to Hatchōbori Station (~5–8 min from the hotel).
  • JR Keiyō Line → Maihama Station ~13 min, direct, runs every ~15 min. Stay on toward Soga / Kaihimmakuhari.
  • At Maihama, take the Disney Resort Line monorail 1 stop → Bayside Station (Hilton is right there). A free shuttle bus from Bayside Station also runs to the hotel door.
  • Drop bags at Hilton Lobby Service (held from 7 AM).
🧭 Train route (transit) 📍 Hatchōbori Stn
💡 Tip: if you take the train but bags are heavy, just take a taxi from Maihama Station → Hilton for the last bit (~¥1,000) instead of the monorail.

🧳 Luggage plan

Cleanest option — no lockers, no fees: bring the bags with you and drop them at Hilton Tokyo Bay’s Lobby Service. The hotel accepts and holds luggage from 7:00 AM, long before the 3 PM room check-in. Drop → explore → come back and check in.
  • Don’t want to carry them at all? MIMARU front desk will hold bags free after your 11 AM checkout — but since you’re heading to the Hilton anyway, dropping at the Hilton is simpler.
  • Coin lockers at Maihama Station — backup if you arrive before you want to go to the hotel. Largest size ~¥800, near the north/south exits.
  • Welcome Center luggage service — the Tokyo Disney Resort Welcome Center near Maihama Station (08:00–15:00) can forward bags to your Hilton room, but the room delivery only completes after 17:00 — so for a same-day check-in, the Lobby Service drop is better.

⏳ Filling the 11 AM – 3 PM gap

After dropping bags you’ll be free around the Maihama/Disney area by ~noon. Easiest options:

Ikspiari mall 🛍️ — best low-effort pick

Right at Maihama Station, 2 min walk · free entry · indoor, air-conditioned

Big shopping + dining complex next to Disney. Easy lunch, kids can roam, souvenirs, a movie theater. Perfect for killing a few hours without committing to a park.
